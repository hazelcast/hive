Modal is a focused, blocking dialog used for high-signal interactions that
require a decision or acknowledgement before the user continues. Use it
sparingly — most flows are better served by inline UI, side panels, or
toasts. Reach for Modal when an action is destructive, irreversible, or
needs a clear yes/no commitment.
